OscMessageParser
================

Simple C++ implementation of OSC message parsing functionality.

Algorithm
|			|s0:idle	|s1:addr	|s2:tag		|s3:param	|s4:stored	|
|adr-rcv	|s1			|s1			|x			|x			|x			|
|adr-don	|x			|s2			|x			|x			|x			|
|adr-err	|x			|s0			|x			|x			|x			|
|tag-rcv	|x			|x			|s2			|x			|x			|
|tag-prm	|x			|x			|s3			|x			|x			|
|tag-non	|x			|x			|s4			|x			|x			|
|tag-err	|x			|x			|s0			|x			|x			|
|prm-rcv	|x			|x			|x			|s3			|x			|
|prm-don	|x			|x			|x			|s4			|x			|
|prm-err	|x			|x			|x			|s0			|x			|

addr
|			|s0:idle	|s1:nonslash|s2:str		|s3:padding	|s4:done	|s5:err	|
|/			|s1			|s5			|s1			|s5			|s1			|s1		|
|ascii		|s5			|s2			|s2			|s5			|s5			|-		|
|\0			|-			|s5			|s3			|-			|-			|-		|
|\0/4		|-			|s5			|s4			|s4			|-			|-		|
|read		|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0		|
|flush		|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0		|

tag
|			|s0:idle	|s1:tag		|s2:padding	|s3:done	|:err	|
|,			|s1			|err		|err		|s1			|s1		|
|i/f/s/b	|err		|-			|err		|err		|-		|
|\0			|-			|s2			|-			|-			|-		|
|\0/4		|-			|s3			|s3			|-			|-		|
|ascii		|err		|err		|err		|err		|-		|
|read		|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0		|
|flush		|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0			|s0		|
